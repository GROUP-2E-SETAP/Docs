Introduction
============
 
**Student Finance Tracker (SFT)** is a finance management app built specifically for students.
It helps you take control of your money through smart budgeting, savings goals, and a
machine-learning-powered spending analyser.
 
.. note::
   This is a Group 2E project, released in 2026.
 
----
 
What does SFT do?
-----------------

   .. list-table::
    :widths: 10 90 
    :header-rows: 0 

    * - **Track spending** 
      - log and categorise transactions. 
    * - **Set savings goals** 
      - define targets and monitor you progress. 
    * - **Budget analysis** 
      - ML-powered insights into your spending patterns. 
    * - **Gamification** 
      - earn XP and level up by hitting goals and staying consistent.
    * - **AI recommendation** 
      - A built in AI ( powered by OpenAI), which helps you find best deals. 

----
 
Who is it for?
--------------
 
SFT is designed for **university and college students** who want a simple, engaging way to
manage their finances — without needing a finance background.
